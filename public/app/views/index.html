What we do

Communicate with Personality on Social Media

We despise brands that betray the very essence of social media by communicating their message through a voice that does not have a hint of personality. In other words - Robot-like.

Social media is a place where brands have a chance of becoming human. An opportunity to see their followers eye-to-eye. Yet is is treated with disgrace with transactional posts that are all but personal.

What pisses us off more is if these brands have products that have immense potential in making an impact. These are the very brands we want to deal with, the only brands we will deal with - the ones that want to make a good impact for the people around it.

That’s what we do. We make sure that brands that do good transform themselves into flag-bearers on social media by communicating to connect with followers, to connect followers to other followers, and create communities that form movements towards a purpose.

And to finally, topple down big brands that are laying waste to the very world we live in, and the people make it.
